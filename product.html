Power Cloud

Today's businesses need data and processing to keep operating. There are many threats to you business operations from a virus, security breach, hardware failures or human error. The key to a quick & complete recovery is an up to date, complete set of your data. WebTRIM Power Cloud is an automated, online & up to the second live backup. This is used to recover from a disaster event ASAP. With Power Cloud your WebTRIM is seamlessly operational.

Power Cloud also includes powerful Business Analytics to give you invaluable insight into the performance of your business. The Analytics can be used by your business coach or accountant to compare against industry statistics to keep you ahead of the pack and assist target setting and forecasting.
